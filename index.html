Wally's Whiskey Advent Calendar 2025

Our 12-Day Whiskey Countdown to Christmas!
The traditional "12 Days of Christmas" run from Dec 25 to Jan 5. But we’re doing it our way: one legendary pour each day from Dec 14 to Christmas Day — because who waits that long for good whiskey?

How to Taste (Blind Style)

  1. On December 14, open bottle marked #1.
  2. Pour, swirl, nose, sip — take mental or written notes (flavor, finish, guesses).
  3. Come back here at 8:00 AM EST to reveal the whiskey.
  4. Repeat daily — one pour, one reveal — until Christmas!

No peeking. Trust your palate. Let the whiskey speak.
